What if Net Ranking and Geography were all that mattered?

The NCAA Basketball tournament gives priority site placement to teams on the top 4 seed lines (16 teams in total). There are eight 1st/2nd round sites, and each site has two "pods" which may feed into different regional sites. Once each site has been filled by two top teams no other top 4 seed can be placed there. I have taken the straight line distance from home gym to potential 1st/2nd round gym of every team in the top 32 of the Net Rankings (as of 2/26/19) and written a program that outputs the closest site available for the top 4 seed lines. Click the button to find out how the site distances might change as teams move up or down the rankings. Iowa State for instance must pass Michigan, Purdue, Wisconsin, and LSU to get Des Moines. But could also be helped by Virginia or Kentucky falling down the rankings.
